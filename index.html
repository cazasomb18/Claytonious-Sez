SCINTILLATING SEQUENCES

First, enter the names for each player and click submit, you will then be able to click on the 'PLAY GAME' button to start the game!

Once the game starts, you will see a sequence of lights and colors flash on the screen, each user will take a turn replicating the sequence.

For Player One:
↑ = up/red
← = left/green
↓ = down/blue
→ = right/yellow

For Player Two:
'W' = up/red
'A' = left/green
'S' = down/blue
'D' = right/yellow

One wrong move and you FAIL!
