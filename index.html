SCINTILLATING SEQUENCES

You will see a sequence of lights and colors flash on the screen, each user will take a turn replicating the sequence.

Player One's arrow keys correspond to the sequence values.

For Player Two:
'W' = (up/red)
'A' = (left/green)
'S' = (down/blue)
'D' = (right/yellow).

One wrong move and you FAIL!
